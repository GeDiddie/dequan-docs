## Multi-timeframe

Switch between **1-second**, **1-minute**, **5-minute**, **15-minute**, **1-hour**, and **daily** candles at any time. The data layer keeps a deep enough history that switching timeframes is instant — no reload, no refetch.

## Inline sparklines on the feed

Both the Pump Zone and the Sniper List embed a tiny sparkline of recent candles directly on the token glyph or row. You can scan a hundred tokens and read the trend on each without opening a single chart.

The sparklines aren't toy renders — they're the same candle data, just compressed into a few pixels of meaningful shape.

## Popout charts

Any chart can be popped out into a floating window that stays onscreen as you move around the app. The popout is a full surface: live ticks, Quick Buy, current PnL, milestone flags, one-tap exits.

This means you can keep the chart of a token you're holding *visible at all times* while you continue scanning the field for the next opportunity. Most other tools force you to choose: study one token or scan the market. Dequan does both.
