Most of Dequan is built around the core trading flow — discovery, charting, execution, exits. The Toolbox is where utilities live that don't fit that flow but matter to active traders.

It is deliberately small. We don't ship clutter. Every tool here exists because traders kept asking for it or because we kept needing it ourselves.

## What's coming

The Toolbox is intentionally a growth surface. We add tools when they earn their keep. Likely future additions:

- **Wallet snapshot exporter** — pull your complete trade history into a CSV/spreadsheet format for tax/PnL analysis.
- **Watchlist alerts** — push notifications when a watched token enters a velocity band or breaks a milestone.
- **Quick-revoke for stale token approvals** — sweep old token approvals that may still be live on your main wallet.
- **Multi-wallet view** — link multiple wallets under one Dequan session for unified scanning and PnL.

These are not promises. They are the kind of thing that ships when we believe it adds real value. Subscribe to the [changelog](/resources/changelog) to see them as they land.

## What's *not* coming

We are not adding:

- **Yield farming dashboards.** Not our domain.
- **NFT trading.** Not our domain.
- **Cross-chain bridging.** Not our domain.
- **Generic wallet management.** That's what your actual wallet is for.

Dequan is a focused tool. The Toolbox stays focused too.
