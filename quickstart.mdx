You don't need an account. You don't need a credit card. You don't need to read a manual. You need a Solana wallet and one minute.

## 1. Open Dequan

<Card title="snipe.dequan.xyz" icon="globe" href="https://snipe.dequan.xyz" horizontal>
  Hosted on Cloudflare's global edge. Loads in any browser, on any device.
</Card>

## 2. Connect a wallet

Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack. One tap, one signature. We never see your private key. There is no "create a Dequan account" step — the wallet **is** your account.

<Note>
The signature you sign during connect is a **proof-of-ownership challenge**, not a transaction. It moves zero SOL and authorises nothing on-chain. It exists so we can prove you control the wallet without holding any keys.
</Note>

## 3. Enter the Pump Zone

This is the home view. Tokens flow across the field as live data arrives. Position on the field tells you everything you need to know:

- **Horizontal** — market cap. Smaller tokens on the left, larger on the right.
- **Vertical** — velocity. Tokens rising fast in price drift upward; tokens cooling drift downward.
- **Glow & motion** — fresh trades flash. Sustained climbers leave a trail.

Your eye will pick out movement before any leaderboard could rank it. That is the entire design.

<Tip>
You can switch into **Sniper List** mode for a traditional analyst view at any time. The same data, presented as a sortable table with charts inline.
</Tip>

## 4. Tap a token

A drawer opens with a live chart, holders breakdown, dev history, recent trades, and a buy panel with **Quick Snipe** preset to your default size.

The instant the drawer opens, Dequan begins preparing the trade in the background — fetching a quote, warming the route, lining up everything execution needs. By the time you choose to buy, almost nothing is left to do.

## 5. Buy

Press **Snipe**. Approve the wallet prompt. Done.

Your transaction is dispatched across multiple landing paths simultaneously. Whichever lane lands first becomes your fill. You get a signature in seconds, often in well under one second.

The token immediately appears as a lane in the **Racing Lanes HUD** — your live position glides forward as price moves, milestones flash as you cross them, and one-tap exits live right on the lane.

## 6. Exit when you're ready

- **One-tap presets** — sell 25%, 50%, 75%, or all of a position from the lane itself.
- **Take Profit Now** — single button, plain English, no menus.
- **Outcome card** — every closed trade ends with a one-screen summary so you can learn from your own pattern.

That is the whole loop. You are now trading on Dequan.

## What just happened, technically

Behind that minute, Dequan's systems quietly:

- Streamed live token metrics from a fan-out pipeline so your browser never opens upstream connections directly.
- Pre-warmed your trade so the click-to-signature path was as short as the network would allow.
- Race-broadcast your signed transaction across multiple landing paths to maximise the odds of landing in the next block.
- Validated the token was not a honeypot before you even saw the chart.
- Watched the pool's liquidity in real time and would have warned you instantly if it drained.
- Logged every checkpoint of the trade so you can see exactly where time was spent.

You didn't see any of that. You shouldn't have to. **Dequan does the engineering so you can do the trading.**
