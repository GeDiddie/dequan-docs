Dequan ships two ways to discover tokens. Both run on the exact same live data pipeline — same metrics, same updates per second, same safety signals. The difference is purely how that data is presented to you.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pump Zone — visualized discovery" icon="radar" href="/pump-zone/overview">
    The live spatial field. Tokens move by market cap and rise by velocity. Built for fast pattern recognition.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sniper List — the legacy method for token discovery" icon="table-list">
    A sortable, filterable table view of the same data. The familiar way to browse tokens.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The Pump Zone is Dequan's reimagined take on token discovery — designed and architected from the ground up to replace the traditional list interface. Instead of rows and columns, the market becomes a live spatial field where momentum is visible at a glance. The patterns that experienced traders spend years learning to spot become obvious in minutes on the Pump Zone.

**We strongly recommend the Pump Zone.** It is the reason Dequan exists.

The Sniper List is included for traders who prefer the familiar table format or need to do side-by-side comparisons. It works — it just doesn't give you the spatial edge.
